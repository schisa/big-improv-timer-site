Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Big Improv Timer handles information when you use the iOS app.

Effective date:

Overview

Big Improv Timer is a simple rehearsal timer for improv performers. The app is designed to collect as little information as possible. There are no user accounts and no in-app social features.

Information We Do Not Collect

Big Improv Timer does not ask you to provide:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files

The app also does not include ads and does not use third-party advertising SDKs or tracking for advertising purposes.

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

When you use the iOS app, Apple and common mobile app services may automatically collect limited technical information to help the developer understand app performance and reliability. This may include:

  • Anonymous usage data, such as which screens are used and for how long
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Basic device information (for example, device model and iOS version)

This information is used in aggregate form to improve the stability and usability of Big Improv Timer. It is not used to personally identify you.

How We Use Information

Any diagnostic or usage information that is available is used solely to:

  • Understand how the app is performing
  • Identify and fix bugs or crashes
  • Make design decisions to improve the app over time

Data Sharing

Big Improv Timer does not sell your data. Limited information may be processed by platform providers (such as Apple) and analytics or crash-reporting tools, to the extent they are enabled for the app. These providers help deliver and improve the app.

Children’s Privacy

Big Improv Timer is intended for general audiences and does not target children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of app-related information by uninstalling the app from your device. You may also be able to control certain analytics or diagnostic settings in your iOS device settings under Privacy and Security.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
