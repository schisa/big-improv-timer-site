About Big Improv Timer

Big Improv Timer was created by Stephen. He used Warp, Sublime, and Procreate to create this app.

Why?

All of my improv teams always have a hassle trying and find a timer that works for our practices. YouTube timer videos have weird ads in the middle of them, the default timer app is too small to read from across the room, and other websites don't prevent the devices from going to sleep mid set. I built this app so my team's didn't have to waste 3 minutes getting a timer set up. I hope you can also get 3 minutes back for your practices using Big Improv Timer!
