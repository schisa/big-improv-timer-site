Big Improv Timer is a bold, easy-to-read rehearsal timer designed for improv teams. Quickly see how much time you have left from across the room.

Set the Big Improv Timer

Use it right in your browser.

How the colors work

Big Improv Timer changes the background color based on how much of your set time is left.

  • Green – more than half of the time is left
  • Orange/Yellow – Less than half of the time is left
  • Red – Less than 10% of the time is left
